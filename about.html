Origin Story

H-Thai-ML is a Los Angeles based Thai restaurant, founded in 2002. The original location was opened in Hollywood as a relaxing stop for LA's top developers to escape the office. Since the success of our Hollywood location, we've since opened two more locations to continue to serve the tech community.
